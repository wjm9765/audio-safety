## Read-first collisions with this project

- **SARSteer (arXiv 2510.17633)** — inference-time refusal-steering *defense* for audio LALMs,
  evaluated on **Qwen2-Audio**. Central premise: Arditi-style steering *derived from audio
  activations fails* ("large distributional gap between audio and text activations"), so they
  use a **text-derived** refusal vector + PCA "safe-space ablation" (FigStep-audio ASR
  51.6%→10.8%). **Our causally-verified native-audio refusal controller (add/ablate both work)
  directly contradicts/refines SARSteer's core negative premise.** Closest competitor AND
  sharpest positioning opportunity — cite and confront head-on. (Acceptance UNVERIFIED; treat
  as preprint.)
- **Benign Fine-Tuning Breaks Safety Alignment in Audio LLMs (2604.16659)** — computes
  layer-wise diff-in-means refusal-direction projections inside audio LLMs (AF3, Qwen2.5-Omni;
  refusal crystallizes ~layers 20–26), but as a fine-tuning-damage diagnostic, **not** causal
  single-direction ablation nor an attack-induced-flip study. Must differentiate explicitly.

## B. Candidate direction-gaps (each: establishes / why-unsolved / fit)

- **Gap 1 — Native, causally-verified audio refusal direction (Arditi-in-audio).** Establishes:
  Arditi 2406.11717, SARSteer 2510.17633 (claims audio-derived steering fails), Benign-FT
  2604.16659. Unsolved: no one extracts a refusal direction from **audio-token** activations and
  shows single-direction **ablation removes / addition induces** refusal in an LALM; SARSteer
  asserts infeasible. Fit: **maximal** — our "refusal controller (add/ablate both work)"; direct
  empirical rebuttal of SARSteer.
- **Gap 2 — Representation-level account of the attack-induced FLIP (not just ASR).** Establishes:
  JALMBench (unexplained audio>text), Acoustic Interference 2605.18168, ReGap 2605.18104. Unsolved:
  audio benchmarks measure the flip behaviorally; no one decomposes whether A(T(H)) **flips** vs
  **attenuates** the refusal axis, with **benign difference-in-differences** (harmful-specific) and
  **audio×text interaction** (audio-specific) controls. Fit: **maximal** — literally our design.
- **Gap 3 — Does the text refusal direction transfer to the audio residual stream, or drift/collapse?**
  Establishes: TGA 2410.12662, CMRM 2410.09047, ShiftDC 2502.13095, ReGap 2605.18104, OmniSteer
  2602.10161. Unsolved: untested in audio whether the *text* refusal axis is the *same* under audio,
  whether audio induces ReGap-style drift/mid-layer collapse that **quantitatively explains**
  JALMBench's gap. Fit: **strong** — audio instantiation of ReGap/TGA; dovetails with our interaction analysis.
- **Gap 4 — "Model still knows" dissociation in audio (probe-behavior gap).** Establishes: HiddenDetect
  2502.14744. Unsolved: no audio analog — does the residual stream still linearly encode
  harmfulness/refusal-intent when it behaviorally complies to A(T(H))? Fit: **strong & distinctive** —
  our **weak AUROC (~0.60) natural readout while add/ablate steering works** *is* the phenomenon
  (causal control ≠ linear decodability).
- **Gap 5 — Causally-grounded steering DEFENSE that restores audio refusal, adaptively stress-tested.**
  Establishes: SARSteer (no causal proof, no adaptive attack), Circuit Breakers 2406.04313 (no audio),
  obfuscated-activations 2412.09565. Unsolved: no audio steering defense built on a causally-verified
  audio direction, evaluated vs an adaptive/obfuscated-activation attacker. Fit: **good** — defense payoff.
- **Gap 6 — Is audio refusal one axis or a cone, and is the flip harmful-specific?** Establishes:
  Concept Cones 2502.17420, "More to Refusal…" 2602.02132, AOR-Bench 2606.21147. Unsolved: in audio,
  do harmful-refusal and benign-over-refusal ride the same axis? Fit: **good** — our **benign DiD** is
  the exact instrument.

**Strategic note (agent):** Gaps **1+2** together = tightest ICLR story — a causally-verified
native-audio refusal direction (Gap 1) + a representation-level decomposition of the attack-induced
flip with harmful- and audio-specificity controls (Gap 2), on a single open model (Qwen2-Audio),
rebutting SARSteer and explaining the gap JALMBench only measures. Gaps 3–4 depth; Gap 5 defense.
